# Source brief (fictional): Northwind Logistics support ops review

> Everything below is invented for this repo's examples. Northwind Logistics
> is not a real company. This is the raw material both example decks were
> built from — compare how much of it actually survives into each deck.

Northwind Logistics runs 11 regional support hubs. In Q3 they piloted an
AI-assisted triage tool in 3 of the 11 hubs, aimed at their highest-volume
ticket category: password resets.

**Numbers from the pilot:**
- 2,860 total support tickets in Q3 across all 11 hubs
- 1,201 of those (42%) were password resets
- Average resolution time before the pilot: 6.2 hours
- Average resolution time in the 3 pilot hubs: 2.1 hours
- Cost per ticket fell from $14.80 to $6.20 in the pilot hubs
- Projected annual savings if rolled out to all 11 hubs: $186,000

**The ask:** budget approval to extend the pilot from 3 hubs to all 11 by the
end of Q1, starting with the password-reset flow since it's the largest
single category. Decision needed by August 15, on the board's Q3 call.

**Audience:** external — this goes to Northwind's board, not our own team.
